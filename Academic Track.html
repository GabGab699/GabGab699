Here are the academic strand that are available in MCPI

We offer:

HUMSS

(Humanities and Social Sciences)
The learning activities are directed towards the development of critical thinking.
HUMSS also focuses on developing your oral communication, media and information, and will hone your skills in reading and writing to a professional level.

STEM

(Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics)
Through the STEM strand, senior high school students are exposed to complex mathematical and science theories and concepts which will serve as a foundation for their college courses.

ABM

(Accountancy, Business and Management)
Taking ABM subjects in senior high school will introduce students to the concepts of financial management, business management, corporate operations, and accounting. Students who select this route will be prepared for colourful careers as managers, accountants, and business owners.

GAS

(General Academic Strand)
The general academic strand encompasses various tasks, behaviors, and experiences that scholars in academic and higher education contexts manage throughout their careers. It includes individual characteristics, contextual factors, active regulation of behavior, career stages, and work and nonwork roles. This caters students who are not yet sure of what course or degree they want to take in college. This strand was designed so that indecisive learners can proceed with any college program.
